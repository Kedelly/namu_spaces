Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does interior design cost in Nairobi?

+

Our services start from KES 15,000 for a consultation and scale depending on the scope of the project. We work with a range of budgets and are always transparent about costs upfront. Contact us for a personalised quote.

Do you work on both residential and commercial projects?

+

Yes. We work on residential spaces (apartments, family homes, villas), commercial spaces (offices, retail, hospitality), and everything in between. Our process adapts to the unique needs of each project type.

How long does an interior design project take?

+

Timelines vary by scope. A consultation takes 2–3 hours. A full room redesign typically takes 4–8 weeks from concept to completion. Larger projects can take 3–6 months. We always provide a realistic timeline in our proposal.

Do I need to buy all new furniture?

+

Not at all. We work with what you have wherever possible, incorporating existing pieces you love into the new design. Our job is to make your space work better — not to sell you things you don't need.

Do you offer 3D visualisations before implementation?

+

Yes. Our 3D Render Designs service provides photorealistic visualisations of your space before any physical work begins. This is especially valuable for clients who want to see exactly what they're getting before committing.

Where are you based and do you travel?

+

We're based in Nairobi, Kenya, and primarily serve the greater Nairobi area including Westlands, Karen, Kilimani, Lavington, Runda, and the CBD. We can travel to other Kenyan cities for larger projects — get in touch to discuss.
